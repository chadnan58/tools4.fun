How to Use the Image Compressor

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the "Upload Image" button to select an image from your device.
  2. Adjust Compression Settings:
    • Set quality level (higher = better quality but larger file)
    • Choose output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
    • Option to preserve metadata (EXIF data)
  3. Compress: Click the "Compress Image" button to process your image.
  4. Compare & Download: View the before/after comparison and click "Download Compressed Image" to save the optimized version.

Tips for Best Results

  • For photos, JPEG at 70-80% quality often provides good balance
  • For graphics with transparency, use PNG
  • WebP format typically offers the best compression
  • Start with 80% quality and adjust as needed
  • Remove metadata for smallest possible files
