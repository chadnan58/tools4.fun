How to Create a Flowchart

  1. Add shapes: Select a shape type, enter text, and click "Add to Canvas"
  2. Position elements: Drag shapes to arrange your flowchart
  3. Connect shapes: Click on a connector dot and drag to another shape
  4. Edit elements: Click on a shape to select it, then change its text or color
  5. Organize: Use different shapes for different flowchart elements
  6. Save your work: Click "Save Flowchart" to download as an image
