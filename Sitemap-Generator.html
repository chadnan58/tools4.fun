How to Use This Sitemap Generator

  1. Enter your website URL to automatically crawl pages (or)
  2. Paste your list of URLs (one URL per line)
  3. Set the change frequency (how often pages are updated)
  4. Set the default priority for pages (0.1 to 1.0)
  5. Click "Generate Sitemap" to create your XML sitemap
  6. Download the file or copy to clipboard
  7. Upload the sitemap.xml to your website root directory
  8. Submit to Google Search Console for better indexing

Why Use a Sitemap?

  • Helps search engines discover your pages
  • Improves SEO and indexing
  • Specifies page priority and update frequency
  • Essential for large websites with many pages
  • Required for video and image SEO
