Security Tips

Use unique passwords for each account. Enable two-factor authentication when available. Never share your passwords.

About This Vault

All passwords are encrypted in your browser and never sent to any server. Your data stays on your device.

How to Use This Password Vault

  1. Enter the service/website name and your username
  2. Create or generate a strong password
  3. Add any optional notes about the account
  4. Click "Save" to store the password securely
  5. View your saved passwords by clicking on them
  6. Use the copy buttons to easily copy usernames or passwords
  7. Export your passwords regularly for backup
