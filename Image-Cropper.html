How to Use Image Cropper

Step 1: Upload

  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop your image
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Max file size: 5MB

Step 2: Crop

  • Drag the crop box to select the area you want
  • Use aspect ratio buttons for specific proportions
  • Adjust zoom and rotation as needed

Step 3: Preview

  • Your cropped image will appear in the preview area
  • Make adjustments if needed
  • Click "Crop Image" to update the preview

Step 4: Download

  • Click "Download Image" to save your cropped photo
  • The image will be saved as a PNG file
  • No registration required - completely free!
