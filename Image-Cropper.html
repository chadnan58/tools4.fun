How to Use the Image Cropper

Upload Image

  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop an image
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • Max file size: 5MB (for demo purposes)

Crop Your Image

  • Drag the corners to adjust the cropping area
  • Use the aspect ratio buttons for specific proportions
  • Click "Rotate" to rotate your image 90 degrees
  • Click "Crop Image" to apply the crop

Download Result

  • After cropping, the download button will activate
  • Click "Download" to save your cropped image
  • The image will be saved in its original format
  • For best results, use high-quality source images

Tips & Tricks

  • Use the free ratio for custom dimensions
  • Square (1:1) is ideal for profile pictures
  • 16:9 is perfect for banners and headers
  • Refresh the page to start over with a new image
