How to Use the Image Filter Tool

Upload Your Photo

  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop your photo
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • Max file size: 5MB (for optimal performance)
  • Your image is processed locally - no server upload

Apply Filters

  • Use preset filters for quick transformations
  • Adjust individual filters with the sliders
  • Combine multiple filters for unique effects
  • See real-time preview of your changes

Preset Filters

  • Clarendon: Brightens and intensifies colors
  • Gingham: Vintage washed-out look
  • Moon: Cool, desaturated tones
  • Lark: Enhances greens and blues
  • Reyes: Warm vintage filter
  • Juno: Warm tones with pink highlights
  • Slumber: Soft, dreamy desaturation

Save & Share

  • Click "Download Image" to save your filtered photo
  • The image will be saved in PNG format
  • Share your creations on social media
  • Use the reset button to start over
