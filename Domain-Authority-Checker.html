Understanding Domain Authority

Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how well a website will rank on search engine result pages. Scores range from 1 to 100, with higher scores corresponding to greater ability to rank.

  • 0-30: Low authority - Needs significant SEO work
  • 30-50: Medium authority - Competitive for some keywords
  • 50-70: Good authority - Strong competitive position
  • 70+: Excellent authority - Among the top websites
