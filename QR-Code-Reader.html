How to Use This QR Code Reader

  1. Camera Scanning:
    • Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
    • Point your camera at a QR code
    • Hold steady until the code is detected
    • Results will appear automatically
  2. Image Upload:
    • Click "Upload Image"
    • Select an image containing a QR code
    • The tool will scan and display the content
  3. View Results: The decoded content will appear in the results box along with the content type

About QR Codes

QR codes can contain different types of information:

  • URLs: Website links that open in your browser
  • Text: Plain text messages or notes
  • Contact Info: vCards that save to your contacts
  • Wi-Fi: Network credentials to connect automatically
  • And more: Emails, phone numbers, SMS messages

Privacy Information

This tool processes all QR codes directly in your browser. No images or scanned data are uploaded to any server - your information stays private on your device.
