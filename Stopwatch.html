How to Use the Stopwatch

1

Start Timing

Click the "Start" button to begin the stopwatch. The timer will begin counting up.

2

Record Laps

Press "Lap" to record the current time without stopping the stopwatch.

3

Pause When Needed

Use "Pause" to temporarily stop the timer. Press "Start" to resume.

4

Reset Everything

Click "Reset" to stop the timer and clear all recorded laps.
