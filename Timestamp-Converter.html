❓ How to Use the Timestamp Converter

This tool allows you to convert between Unix timestamps and human-readable dates. Unix time (also known as POSIX time or epoch time) is a system for describing a point in time, defined as the number of seconds that have elapsed since 00:00:00 Coordinated Universal Time (UTC), Thursday, 1 January 1970.

1

Convert Timestamp to Date

Enter a Unix timestamp (in seconds) in the first input field. Select your preferred timezone and click "Convert to Date".

2

Convert Date to Timestamp

Select a date and time using the datetime picker. Choose your timezone and click "Convert to Timestamp".

3

Current Timestamp

The current Unix timestamp is displayed at the bottom of the converter for reference.
