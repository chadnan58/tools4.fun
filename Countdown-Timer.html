How to Use This Countdown Timer

  1. Set the hours, minutes, and seconds for your countdown
  2. Or use the preset buttons for common timer durations
  3. Click "Start" to begin the countdown
  4. Use "Pause" to temporarily stop the timer
  5. Use "Reset" to stop and reset the timer to your initial values
  6. Choose an alarm sound that will play when the timer completes

Tip: The timer will continue counting down even if you switch browser tabs.
