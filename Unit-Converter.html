How to Use & Reference
1

Select Conversion Type

Choose the category of units you want to convert (length, weight, etc.) from the tabs.

2

Enter Value

Type the value you want to convert in the "Convert From" input field.

3

Select Units

Choose the unit you're converting from and the unit you're converting to from the dropdown menus.

4

Convert

Click the "Convert Units" button to see the result. You can also swap units with the circular button.
