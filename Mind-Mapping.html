How to Create a Mind Map

  1. Start with the central idea: Set "Node Type" to "Central Idea" and type your main topic
  2. Add nodes: Click "Add Node" to place your central idea on the canvas
  3. Add child nodes: Change "Node Type" to "Child Node", type your subtopic, and click "Add Node"
  4. Position nodes: Drag nodes to organize your mind map
  5. Edit nodes: Click on a node to select it, then change its text or color
  6. Save your work: Click "Save Map" to download your mind map as an image

Tip: Use different colors for different branches to visually organize your ideas!
