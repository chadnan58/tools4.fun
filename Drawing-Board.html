How to Use the Drawing Board

1

Select Drawing Tool

Choose from pencil, brush, eraser, shapes, or text tool from the toolbar.

2

Choose Color & Size

Pick a color from the palette and select your brush or line thickness.

3

Start Drawing

Click and drag on the canvas to draw. Release to finish your stroke.

4

Save Your Artwork

Download your creation as PNG or JPG image when finished.
