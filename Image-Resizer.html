How to Use the Image Resizer

  1. Upload Your Image: Click the "Upload Image" button to select an image from your device.
  2. Set Dimensions: Enter your desired width and height in pixels. The tool will automatically maintain the aspect ratio by default.
  3. Adjust Settings:
    • Choose whether to maintain aspect ratio (recommended)
    • Toggle high quality preservation (enabled by default)
  4. Resize: Click the "Resize Image" button to process your image.
  5. Preview & Download: View your resized image in the preview section and click "Download Resized Image" to save it to your device.

Tips for Best Results

  • For profile pictures, common sizes are 200x200px or 400x400px
  • Website headers typically range from 1024x300px to 1920x500px
  • Social media posts vary by platform (Instagram: 1080x1080px, Facebook: 1200x630px)
  • Maintain aspect ratio to prevent image distortion
