How to Use This Tool

  1. Paste your content in the text area above (minimum 100 words for best results)
  2. Optionally enter a focus keyword to track its specific density
  3. Choose whether to ignore common words (recommended) and case sensitivity
  4. Click "Analyze Keyword Density" to process your content
  5. Review the results to see:
    • Total word count and unique keywords
    • Overall keyword density percentage
    • Focus keyword density (if specified)
    • Detailed keyword frequency table

Understanding Keyword Density

  • 1-2% density: Optimal range for most keywords
  • Below 0.5%: Keyword may be underused
  • 2-3% density: Approaching over-optimization
  • Above 3% density: Likely keyword stuffing (penalized by search engines)
  • Focus keywords should typically be in the 1-2% range
  • Natural content varies - these are guidelines, not strict rules
