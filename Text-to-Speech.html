How to Use
1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste the text you want to convert to speech in the input box. There's no limit to the amount of text you can convert.

2

Select Voice & Settings

Choose from available voices and adjust the speech rate and pitch to your preference. Preview voices to find the perfect match.

3

Click "Play" to hear your text spoken aloud. Use pause and stop controls as needed. You can download the audio as an MP3 file.

4

Download & Share

Download the generated speech as an MP3 file to use in presentations, videos, or for accessibility purposes.
