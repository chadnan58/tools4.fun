How to Use the Collage Maker

Upload Photos

  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop your photos
  • Select at least 2 photos to create a collage
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
  • Max file size: 5MB per image

Select Template

  • Choose from 5 different collage templates
  • Each template has a different layout for your photos
  • Try different templates to find your favorite

Arrange Photos

  • Click on a placeholder in the preview to assign a photo
  • Select a photo from your uploaded images
  • Adjust spacing and corner radius as needed
  • Change background color for your collage

Download & Share

  • Click "Download Collage" to save your creation
  • The collage will be saved as a high-quality PNG file
  • Share your collage on social media or with friends
  • Use the reset button to start a new collage
