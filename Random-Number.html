How to Use the Random Number Generator

  1. Set your range - Enter the minimum and maximum values for your random numbers
  2. Specify quantity - Choose how many random numbers you want to generate
  3. Choose uniqueness - Check the box if you want all numbers to be unique (no duplicates)
  4. Click "Generate Numbers" - Your random numbers will appear instantly
  5. Copy results - Use the "Copy to Clipboard" button to save your numbers
