How to Use the Text Summarizer

Input Your Text

  • Copy and paste the text you want to summarize
  • Our tool works with articles, essays, reports, and documents
  • Maximum text length: 10,000 characters
  • Text is processed locally - no server upload required

Adjust Settings

  • Use the slider to set your desired summary length
  • Choose between different summary styles:
    • General: Balanced summary
    • Bullet Points: Key points in list format
    • Paragraph: Flowing paragraph summary
    • Technical: Focuses on technical terms
    • Simple Language: Easy-to-understand summary

Generate Summary

  • Click "Summarize Text" to process your content
  • Our AI identifies key sentences and main ideas
  • See word count reduction and reading time saved
  • Copy the summary with the "Copy Summary" button

Tips for Best Results

  • For long documents, summarize in sections
  • Use bullet point style for meeting notes
  • Adjust summary length based on your needs
  • Use technical style for research papers
  • Try different styles to see which works best
