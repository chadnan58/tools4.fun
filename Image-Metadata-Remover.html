How to Use Metadata Remover

Step 1: Upload

  • Click on the upload area or drag and drop your image
  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WebP
  • Max file size: 5MB

Step 2: Analyze

  • The tool will scan your image for metadata
  • Detected metadata will be displayed
  • Common metadata includes location, camera info, and timestamps

Step 3: Remove

  • Click "Remove Metadata" to clean your image
  • The cleaned image will appear on the right
  • All personal metadata will be permanently removed

Step 4: Download

  • Download your privacy-safe image
  • The image will be saved as a PNG file
  • No registration required - completely free!

Why Remove Metadata?

Photos taken with smartphones and digital cameras contain hidden metadata (EXIF data) that can include:

  • GPS location coordinates
  • Date and time the photo was taken
  • Camera model and settings
  • Software used to edit the photo
  • Thumbnail previews

Removing this metadata helps protect your privacy when sharing photos online.
