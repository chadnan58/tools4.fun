What Are Meta Tags?

Meta tags are snippets of text that describe a page's content. They don't appear on the page itself, but in the page's HTML code. Search engines use them to understand what your page is about and display it in search results.

Why They Matter

Proper meta tags can:

  • Improve your search engine rankings
  • Increase click-through rates from search results
  • Control how your content appears when shared
