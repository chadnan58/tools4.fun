How to Use the Image Editor

Uploading Images

Click the "Upload Image" button to select an image from your device. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and GIF.

Cropping

Click the "Crop" button and drag the selection area on your image. Press enter to apply the crop.

Resizing

Click "Resize" and enter your desired dimensions in pixels. The aspect ratio is maintained automatically.

Adjustments

Use the brightness and contrast sliders to enhance your image. Click "Reset" to revert to original values.

Compression

Adjust the quality slider to optimize file size. Lower values create smaller files with reduced quality.

Saving

Choose your output format and click "Download" to save the edited image to your device.
