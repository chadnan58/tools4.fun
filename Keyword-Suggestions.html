How to Use the Keyword Suggestions Tool

Get Started

  • Enter your main keyword or phrase in the input field
  • Select an industry or category to refine your results
  • Click "Generate Keywords" to get suggestions
  • Our AI analyzes millions of search queries for relevant ideas

Analyze Results

  • Review keyword suggestions with search volume and competition data
  • Use tabs to filter by high volume, low competition, or long-tail keywords
  • Check the keyword cloud for related terms and variations
  • Export results for further analysis

Effective Keyword Research

  • Focus on keywords with buyer intent for better conversions
  • Target long-tail keywords (3+ words) for less competition
  • Consider search volume vs. competition ratio
  • Group related keywords for content clusters

Implementation Tips

  • Use primary keywords in page titles and headings
  • Incorporate secondary keywords naturally in content
  • Create content around question-based keywords
  • Update existing content with new keyword opportunities
  • Track keyword rankings over time
